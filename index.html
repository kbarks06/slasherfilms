About This Site

Slasher films are more than just gore and screams — they’re a cultural phenomenon that has evolved over decades, carving out iconic villains, unforgettable final girls, and franchises that just won’t die.

On this site, you'll explore the rich and chilling history of slasher cinema, including:

  • Legendary Characters: Dive into profiles of killers like Michael Myers, Freddy Krueger, and Ghostface — and their brave survivors.
  • Famous Franchises: From Halloween to Friday the 13th to Scream, uncover what made these series timeless (and terrifying).
  • Notable Actors: Learn about the actors behind the masks — and those who brought unforgettable performances to life on screen.

Whether you're a lifelong horror fan or a curious newcomer, this site will guide you through the thrills, kills, and chills of slasher films.
