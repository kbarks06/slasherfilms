Jamie Lee Curtis

Known for: Halloween Series

Often dubbed the original "Scream Queen," Jamie Lee Curtis rose to fame with her iconic role as Laurie Strode. Her performance helped define the final girl trope in horror films.

Neve Campbell

Known for: Scream Series

As Sidney Prescott, Neve Campbell brought strength and vulnerability to the genre, leading the Scream franchise and redefining what it means to be a modern final girl.
